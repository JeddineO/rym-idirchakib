TOUR OF ALGERIA

Algeria Tour

Algiers is the political, administrative, and economic capital of Algeria. It is also known as El Bahdja (the cheerful), El Mahroussa (the well-guarded), El Djazaer, or even the “White City” – Alger La Blanche.

We are organizing a day tour for our guests who will be traveling from overseas. Please liaise with Rym if you are interested in taking part – we’d love to show you around the gorgeous capital!

Rich in history, culture, and architecture, the city is infused with historical remnants of the civilizations that have prevailed, creating a harmonic contrast between modernity and the past. The Casbah, the Ketchaoua mosque, the Notre-Dame d’Afrique basilica, and many more historic buildings are just a few incredible sites you can visit in Algiers – which have become symbols of the city’s long and rich history.
