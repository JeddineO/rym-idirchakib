  • What to Wear?

    January 1, 2016

    The dress code is formal of course, however we’d love to see you arrive in your own traditional attire (not limited to Algerian traditional attire) – no matter where in the world you’re from!
    As we are aware that in most traditional Algerian weddings, women enjoy wearing multiple outfits to ceremonies – but we’d like to kindly ask if guests can refrain from bringing large luggage into the ball room.
    If you plan on wearing multiple dresses, kindly coordinate with Rym to organize a changing room where you may store your dresses and other belongings.

  • Wedding Gifts

    Since our ceremony is taking place in Algiers, where both the bride and groom do not currently reside, we kindly ask of you to refrain from bringing any large gifts to the wedding venue.
    Your presence is our greatest gift!
    Cash gifts are also welcome but not expected.
