Location

The historical Hôtel Saint-George Located in the El Mouradia district of Algiers

As the bride and groom are two Algerian nationals who couldn’t be more proud of their cultural roots, we thought it would be fitting to host our ceremony in the beautiful capital of Algiers – and in the city’s most historic hotel, the Hotel Saint George.

Check out this video for an exciting preview of the venue.
Inaugurated in 1889, it had a number prestigious guests that include many famous historical figures – like Malcom X, Edith Piaf, Simone de Beauvoir, and Winston Churchill.
