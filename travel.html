Travel & Accommodation

Travel

If you aren’t an Algerian passport holder, please keep in mind that flights need to be booked before you can submit your visa application.

Here are some flight options you can look into if you’re traveling from overseas to Algiers Houari Boumediene Airport:

  • Qatar Airways offers via Hamad International Airport (Doha)
  • Emirates via Dubai International Airport
  • Turkish Airlines via Istanbul Airport
  • Air France via Paris Orly Airport
  • Lufthansa via Frankfurt Airport
  • British Airways via London Heathrow Airport

Getting Around in Algiers

The YASSIR application in Algiers is similar to Uber, LYFT, and Careem.

But if you’d like to arrange a different mode of transportation – please liaise with Rym!

VISA

If you are not a holder of an Algerian (Tunisian, or Moroccan) passport, then you’ll need a visa to travel to Algiers.

It’s a tedious process which can take up to a week.

But wherever you are based in the world, your visa application must be submitted via your local Algerian Embassy or Consulate.

For US residents, here’s what you have to do to make sure your visa gets processed on time:

Residents of California and New York

You can submit your application via the Consulate General in New York.

Documents required:

  • Visa application form to be filled out on-screen, printed, dated and hand signed;
  • One (1) recent standard ID photo with white background.
  • A Confirmed hotel reservation or holiday booking through a travel agency, or an invitation letter from an Algeria resident person (must be notarized by City Hall in Algiers and his ID attached), or an invitation letter from an Algerian US resident issued at a notary public office. (Please reach out to Rym if you’re in need of one of these letters)
  • A Copy of an airline itinerary;
  • Last statement of financial resources or proof of income (pay stub, bank statement…);
  • The original passport and a copy of the main photo page (passport must be valid for at least 6 months and contains a minimum of two (2) blank pages);
  • Proof of address (Valid Driver's License, Utility Bill, Bank or credit card statement, insurance policy or bill…);
  • For a faster and safer return of your documents, provide a Priority Mail Express return envelope.

Residents of Paris, France

Please click the link below: https://consulat-paris-algerie.fr/visas/

For all other countries/nationalities – please liaise with Rym if you need help with your visa application!
